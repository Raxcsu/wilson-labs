# Wilson Labs

**Unlocking life-saving molecules from coral ecosystems through AI, synthetic biology, and conservation.**

## What We Do

Wilson Labs is building a biotech discovery platform that turns marine biodiversity into scalable therapeutics. We combine marine biodiversity data, AI-driven molecule prediction, and synthetic biology manufacturing to bridge the gap between ocean chemistry and clinical medicine.

Coral reef ecosystems contain some of the most chemically diverse molecules on Earth — yet fewer than a handful ever become medicines. We're changing that.

## Our Approach

We partner with conservation organisations to collect minimal coral samples using non-invasive methods. From there, our platform predicts which compounds have the highest pharmaceutical potential, manufactures them sustainably through synthetic biology, and moves the most promising candidates into partnerships with pharmaceutical companies.

Every discovery feeds back into our models, making the next one faster and more accurate.
